How we met

From the time she went on to the dias at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College,on the day of admission counseling for Institute Level Seats her name sounded familiar for some reason.

Subsequently, I also joined TSEC In the later CAP Rounds, in fall of 2008, I saw Tejal for the first time at Bandra Station.It was drizzling very light in Mumbai that time. It was at that very moment I fell for her. Bombay rains make you feel special.

"By god, One Flash, Ek Chamakh, Usse dekhte hi Pyaar ho gaya tha. "

Took me 3 years to talk to her, slowly we started talking. Then Bangalore happened, and then East Coast.

From almost getting arrested in Whitefield, Bangalore to proposing her at the magnificent Manhattan Skyline, All of the last 11 years have been an roller coaster ride for us.

But now we are here, and She is the Reena Malhotra to Maddy and Andie Anderson to Benjamin Barry. We have been very close throughout this whole journey, the rest is now history, and we will look forward to the new beginnings.
